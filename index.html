Meet the Team

Hi, I'm Dr. Sarah Chen —
Your Phoenix Dentist

I grew up in Phoenix and have spent 18 years building a practice rooted in something simple: treating every patient the way I'd want my own family treated. I started Luminate Dental because I believed dentistry could feel less clinical and more human.

My team and I combine the latest technology — same-day crowns, 3D imaging, digital scans — with a slow-down, listen-first philosophy. We explain every step, answer every question, and never rush you into a treatment plan you're unsure about.

Whether this is your first visit in years or you're ready for a complete smile transformation, you're in the right place. We'd love to meet you.
